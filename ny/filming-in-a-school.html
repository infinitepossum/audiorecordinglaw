Filming at schools and school events — New York

Not legal advice. General information only; laws change and outcomes depend on facts.

Quick answer: Schools involve minors and heightened privacy rules. Even at public schools, filming may be limited by policy, safety rules, and privacy laws.

SettingSchool campus / school event
VideoOften restricted by policy; may require permission
AudioConsent rules apply; extra caution with minors

Why it’s different

Schools often restrict recording to protect students and maintain safety.

Even if you’re in a public facility, you may be in a controlled-access or policy-limited area.

Safer approach

Ask the school/organizer for written permission for any planned filming.

If you must record, keep it to your own child/group and avoid capturing other students.
