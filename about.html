About

This website provides general, educational information about audio recording and filming rules in the United States. The goal is to make common questions easier to navigate with plain-language summaries and links to reputable sources.

What this site does

  • Organizes common recording/filming scenarios by state
  • Summarizes typical rules and common exceptions in plain language
  • Links to third-party resources so you can verify details

What this site does not do

  • It does not provide legal advice
  • It does not interpret laws for your specific facts
  • It does not replace official statutes or a licensed attorney

Important: Laws and court interpretations change. Outcomes depend on facts. If something matters, verify with official sources or a qualified attorney.
